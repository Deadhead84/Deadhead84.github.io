Oil Changes

Replace your engine oil and filter, top off all fluids except brake fluid (unless requested), set tires pressure, perform multi-point inspection on vehicle.

Brakes

We offer an exntensive brake replacement service. We replace the brake pads, clean and lubricate your caliper slide pins, inspect calipers for leaks or damage, and inspect rotors for damage or wear. We recommend that you resurface the rotors if you are experiencing any noise or vibraitons while braking.

Timing Belts

Timing belts require us to dissassemble the front of the engine. Behind the timing cover there is a belt that synronizes the camshafts to your crankshaft. We recommend that it is replaced every 105,000 miles. If the belt snaps it could possibly damage valves and other components resulting in a very expensive repair. We replace your timing belt, water pump, coolant, and drive belts. Often the timing belt tensioner is leaking and will need to be replaced for just the price of the part.
